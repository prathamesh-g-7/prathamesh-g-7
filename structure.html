Registration

Above picture shows the Registration Process

You need to register yourself as a buyer only once . Our system will generate a Unique Identification Id. and will send it to your registered Mobile Number. Then onwards you will be able to purchase any of our products from our authorized Franchisees. With a reference of your Unique Identification Id.

IBD LOGIN (Independent Business Distributor)

Above picture shows the Login Process

By using the Unique Identification Id. and Password which you received at the time of registration should be used to log into the IBD account. After login you will be able to Order / Purchase Any of the Products of Your Choice from our Authorised Franchisees.

IBD Agreement

This is the official Aggrement in above picture

When you successfully login with your ID then you have to read this aggrement and understand the terms and conditions of company. After this, you can go for your KYC Process.

KYC

Above picture shows the KYC Process

Your KYC (Know Your Customer) compliance is necessary as you are now entitled to earn the Distributorship benefits as per the Incentive Plan.
